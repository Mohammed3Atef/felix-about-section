Living your best life starts with your health.

If you’re healthy, you’ll have energy and confidence to chase your dreams and savour every moment.

But that’s easier said than done.

For many Canadians, the path to good health is littered with obstacles: no family doctor; long wait times; even stigma and shame.

And, every health problem is treated the same way. “One size fits all” – except it doesn’t fit anyone.

We think it’s time for a better way.

Company

Felix is Canada’s first truly integrated healthcare platform. We provide on-demand treatment for everyday health needs like weight loss, mental health, sexual health, and more. 

Founded in 2019, our digital-first approach to healthcare includes everything from diagnosis to prescription — all accessible from the comfort of home.
